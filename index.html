What is this game?

This is a Snake & Ladder board inspired game where you can play with 2 or up to 4 players. The goal is to reach the end while landing on special squares triggers financial scam quiz questions. Answer correctly to move ahead or you'll be sent back!

How to play

Roll the dice according to your turn and move your piece. Land on a special square to answer a quiz. Correct = move forward. Wrong = go back. First to reach square 30 wins!
