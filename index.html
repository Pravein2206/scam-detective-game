What is this game?

A Snake & Ladder inspired game for 2–4 players. Land on ❓ squares to answer financial scam quiz questions. Correct = move forward. Wrong = go back. First to reach 🏆 wins!

How to play

Roll the dice on your turn and move your piece. Answer correctly to get a bonus 3 steps forward. Answer wrong and go back 3 steps. Watch out!
